Summer Programs

Every summer, Butler Music School holds special, week-long band camps for students to experience what it's like to be in a rock band. Students spend a week learning 5 to 6 songs, practicing together under the guidance of our talented instructors. At the end of the week, we throw a concert to show off their hard work.

We haven't started accepting signups for this year's band camp yet, but keep an eye on this page, our facebook, or sign up for our mailing list to stay updated!
