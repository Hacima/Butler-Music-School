Summer Programs

Every summer, Butler Music School holds special, week-long group events that our students can sign up for. These provide a structured, guided opportunity for students to see what it's like to be in a band and obtain valuable teamwork experience. These programs can be a great chance to make friends and collaborate with fellow musicians.
