Our Instructors

Cayla Butler
Piano • Voice

Ms. Cayla has been playing music as long as she can remember. She began performing at a young age and hasn't stopped since. She took many piano and vocal lessons and has always had a drive to do what she loves which is performing and composing music. She began teaching at the music school in 2005 and became the owner in 2010. She teaches vocal and piano lessons and currently performs as the featured artist in the Easy Street Band.

Scott Musick
Drums • Guitar

In 1980, Scott Musick and Michael Been formed The Call and enjoyed much critical and popular success throughout the next decade while being signed to three major record labels in the U.S. and abroad. Scott has also toured the world performing with great artists such as Peter Gabriel, Kris Kristopherson, Elvis Costello, Delbert McClinton, The Band, and many others. Musick's current songs are a departure from the The Call's sound, with a more acoustic based style that winds around Americana to acoustic rock, and even a bit of Gospel.

Micah Obal
Guitar • Bass • Piano

Micah Obal has been in music lessons of one kind or another since he was 8 years old. He is proficient in many styles of music, including jazz, modern & classic rock, blues, metal, classical, as well as contemporary & traditional worship. He is the worship leader for Heatherridge Baptist Church, and the lead guitarist for a local symphonic rock band. Formally educated at TCC, Micah has a strong background in music theory, and over 7 years of experience teaching students of all ages.
